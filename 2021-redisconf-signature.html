Instruction on how to add the Rediscover Magazine banner to your current email signature

If you already have the current Redis Labs logo in your signature: 1. Drag (2021-redisconf-signature.html) to your browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, ETC). 2. Highlight and copy just the banner if your name and details are already set up. 3. Open your email and go to settings. Find Signature section, delete the old Redis Labs logo. Go up to the Edit menu and paste. 4. Save your signature.

If you do NOT have any Redis Labs logo in your signature: 1. Drag (2021-redisconf-signature.html) to your browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, ETC). 2. Highlight and copy the name, details, and banner set-up if you do not have any of it. 3. Open your email and go to settings. Find Signature section, add a new signature and paste the content. 4. Save your signature.
